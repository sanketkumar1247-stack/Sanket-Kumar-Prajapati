FEATURED PUBLISHED BOOK

India on the Global Stage: A Political Journey Through the Decade (2014-2024)

This scholarly work provides an in-depth political and strategic analysis of India's foreign policy evolution, diplomatic shifts, and emerging global posture over the transformative decade from 2014 to 2024. It examines India's engagement with multilateral organizations, global governance, strategic autonomy, and flexible minilateral alignments in the Indo-Pacific and European contexts.
