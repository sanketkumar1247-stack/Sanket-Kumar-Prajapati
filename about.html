PhD Scholar · JNU

Sanket Kumar Prajapati · Centre for European Studies, JNU

My doctoral research at the Centre for European Studies, Jawaharlal Nehru University is focused on investigating EU regulatory entrepreneurship, analyzing the global "Brussels Effect", and mapping comparative digital sovereignty frameworks between India and Europe.

Prior to initiating my doctoral program, I completed my training in Political Science at the University of Delhi, finishing both my M.A. and B.A. (Hons.) with high academic honors. I am a recipient of the prestigious UGC Junior Research Fellowship (JRF) and have cleared the National Eligibility Test (NET).

In addition to my academic research, I have a deep commitment to teaching, having designed and delivered remote Indian Polity modules for thousands of UPSC and State civil services (MPPSC) aspirants. I am a native speaker of Hindi and professionally fluent in English.
